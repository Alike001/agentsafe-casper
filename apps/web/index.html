HOW AUTHORITY IS BOUNDED

The agent gets a narrow permission, not control of a wallet.

  1. 01
    Describe intent

    Set the agent, allowed services, limits, budget, approval threshold, and expiry.

  2. 02
    Compile and validate

    AI may draft the policy. Canonical rules and deterministic checks decide whether it is valid.

  3. 03
    Approve with wallet

    The owner reviews the exact mandate and signs the Casper transaction client-side.

  4. 04
    Enforce every action

    Paid requests are checked against active authority before signing or settlement.

SECURITY BOUNDARY

The AI is never the authority.

The LLM cannot hold keys, sign transactions, or make the final allow/block decision.

Policies are wallet-owned, actions are replay-checked, and sensitive prompts or API results stay off-chain.

Final-round disclosure: Testnet-only, unaudited, and not suitable for custody or mainnet funds.
